About the Author

D. H. Wagstaff | Author

I was adopted as a child and spent much of my life knowing very little about my biological heritage. A DNA test later revealed that my ancestry traced to the Balkan region, sparking a deep interest in the history, culture, and people of Albania. While The Last Dispensation of Kings is entirely a work of fiction, that personal journey of discovering identity and heritage became one of the inspirations behind the novel.

D. H. Wagstaff is a novelist and software developer whose stories explore identity, legacy, family, and the extraordinary choices ordinary people make when history unexpectedly intersects with their lives. He enjoys researching history, technology, and the human stories that connect them, bringing those interests together in character-driven novels filled with suspense and intrigue.
